Product Description

Powerslide Zoom Black 100 Inline Urban Skates


Brand new in box! Please measure your feet and check the company size chart in the photos to insure a perfect fit.



The Powerslide One Zoom 100 is the little brother of the Zoom Pro inline skate. In contrast to the Pro skate the Zoom model is equipped with an aluminum frame fixed by rivets to the shell. However, we have brought the Zoom into the modern age by adding a 3-wheel frame. This is the first 3-wheel skate to fall into the entry level market and it is a great one to start with. The sturdy shell houses a comfortable liner which covers two sizes thanks to the elastic material in the toe area. Paired with riveted on one-piece 3-wheel frame, long lasting Hurricane 100mm / 82A PU wheels and WCD ABEC 7 bearings the Zoom 100 won't let you down. The stylish and simple black color way is timeless making this a great freeskate to start your 3-wheel adventure. Available for shipping or in store pick up!
